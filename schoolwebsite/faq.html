Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our school

What are the school admission requirements?

Admissions require a completed application form, previous school records, and an entrance assessment. Visit our admissions page or contact our admissions office for more details.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer sports, music, debate, science club, and various cultural activities. New activities are added based on student interest each semester.

What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM Monday through Friday. Extended care is available until 5:30 PM for registered students.
